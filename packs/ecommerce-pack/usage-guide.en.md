# Ecommerce Skill Pack Usage Guide

This guide explains how to use the 6 core skills in the Ecommerce Skill Pack, how they work together, what inputs to prepare, and how to review outputs before publishing them.

## 1. Pack Overview

The Ecommerce Skill Pack is a reusable AI workflow pack for ecommerce sellers and operators. It helps turn product facts, market notes, competitor observations, and review excerpts into structured ecommerce work products:

- Product positioning
- Competitor analysis
- Review analysis
- Product listing optimization
- Ad copy generation
- Product image brief generation

The pack is designed for Amazon, Shopify, Etsy, TikTok Shop, independent stores, and social commerce workflows.

## 2. Recommended Workflow

Use the skills in this order when launching or improving a product:

1. `product-positioning-analyzer`
2. `competitor-product-analysis`
3. `review-analysis-skill`
4. `product-listing-optimizer`
5. `ad-copy-generator`
6. `product-image-brief-generator`

This order keeps the work grounded. Positioning defines the buyer and angle, competitor analysis reveals market gaps, review analysis provides buyer language, listing optimization turns the strategy into a product page, ad copy tests demand, and image briefs translate the same message into visuals.

## 3. Skill Details

### 3.1 product-positioning-analyzer

Purpose: define the product's target customer, buying motivation, pain points, value, differentiation, and messaging angle.

Best used when:

- Planning a new product launch
- Clarifying a product's main selling point
- Preparing strategy for listing, ads, and images

Prepare these inputs:

- Product and category
- Target customer
- Price or price range
- Competitors
- Differentiators
- Use cases
- Brand tone
- Sales channel

Expected outputs:

- Target customer profile
- Buyer motivation
- Core pain points
- Functional and emotional value
- Primary selling point
- Secondary selling points
- Messaging angles
- Risks and weak points

Review checklist:

- The target customer is specific.
- The primary selling point is clear.
- Assumptions are not presented as facts.
- No certifications, reviews, sales numbers, or performance metrics are invented.

### 3.2 competitor-product-analysis

Purpose: analyze a competitor product page and turn observed strengths, weaknesses, and review signals into opportunities.

Best used when:

- Comparing a product against direct competitors
- Finding listing gaps
- Planning differentiation
- Preparing ad and image angles

Prepare these inputs:

- Competitor product name, title, price, and platform
- Competitor bullet points and description
- Competitor review excerpts
- Competitor image notes
- Our product notes

Expected outputs:

- Competitor positioning
- Target customer assumption
- Main selling points
- Pricing strategy
- Page strengths and weaknesses
- Review-based opportunities
- Differentiation opportunities
- Listing, ad, and image suggestions

Review checklist:

- Observations come from provided competitor data.
- Assumptions are clearly marked.
- The output does not copy competitor wording.
- Recommendations are specific and actionable.

### 3.3 review-analysis-skill

Purpose: turn provided review excerpts into themes, complaints, motivations, product improvements, listing improvements, ad angles, and FAQ opportunities.

Best used when:

- Reviewing customer feedback
- Understanding negative review patterns
- Mining buyer language for listing and ads
- Planning product improvements

Prepare these inputs:

- Product category
- Platform
- Positive reviews
- Negative reviews
- Neutral reviews
- Competitor reviews
- Product facts

Expected outputs:

- Top positive and negative themes
- Buyer motivations
- Usage scenarios
- Repeated complaints
- Product improvement suggestions
- Listing and ad suggestions
- FAQ opportunities
- Review summary table

Review checklist:

- Only supplied reviews are analyzed.
- No ratings, quotes, or sentiment are invented.
- High-frequency and low-frequency issues are separated.
- No review manipulation is suggested.

### 3.4 product-listing-optimizer

Purpose: optimize ecommerce listing copy by turning product facts into titles, benefits, bullets, descriptions, keywords, FAQs, and A/B variants.

Best used when:

- Creating a new listing
- Rewriting an old listing
- Improving Amazon bullets
- Drafting Shopify or Etsy product copy

Prepare these inputs:

- Product name and category
- Target platform
- Target customer
- Main use case
- Key features
- Materials or specs
- Price range
- Brand tone
- Competitor notes
- Language
- Compliance constraints

Expected outputs:

- Product positioning summary
- Optimized title
- Short title alternatives
- Key benefits
- Feature-to-benefit conversion
- Bullet points
- Product description
- Keyword suggestions
- FAQ opportunities
- Risky claims to avoid
- A/B testing variants

Review checklist:

- Features are translated into buyer benefits.
- The listing fits the target platform.
- Claims are supported by product facts.
- No fake reviews, fake certifications, or absolute guarantees are included.

### 3.5 ad-copy-generator

Purpose: generate platform-aware ecommerce ad hooks, headlines, primary text, CTAs, angle variants, and A/B testing plans.

Best used when:

- Testing Meta, TikTok, Google, Amazon, or marketplace ads
- Creating multiple hook variants
- Comparing pain-point, scenario, comparison, and promotion angles

Prepare these inputs:

- Product
- Platform
- Target customer
- Core pain point
- Main benefit
- Offer
- Tone
- Restrictions
- Landing page
- Language

Expected outputs:

- Hook options
- Short and long headlines
- Primary text versions
- CTA options
- Pain-point angle
- Scenario angle
- Comparison angle
- Promotion angle
- A/B testing plan
- Risky wording to avoid

Review checklist:

- Copy style matches the platform.
- Offers and discounts are real.
- No fake scarcity or urgency is used.
- No unsupported performance, health, legal, financial, or certification claims are included.

Purpose: create actionable briefs for ecommerce product images, including main images, lifestyle images, feature images, size/spec images, comparison images, problem-solution images, and A+ content.

Best used when:

- Planning a product image set
- Briefing designers or photographers
- Refreshing Amazon, Shopify, or Etsy visuals
- Turning listing strategy into visual assets

Prepare these inputs:

- Product
- Platform
- Target customer
- Key features
- Main benefits
- Materials or specs
- Brand style
- Image count
- Competitor image notes
- Compliance restrictions

Expected outputs:

- Main image brief
- Lifestyle image brief
- Feature image brief
- Size/spec image brief
- Comparison image brief
- Problem-solution image brief
- A+ content image brief
- Image text suggestions
- Shot list

Review checklist:

## 4. How to Prepare Good Inputs

Better inputs produce safer and more useful outputs. Before running a skill, collect:

- Confirmed product facts
- Exact specs and materials
- Real platform constraints
- Real customer review excerpts
- Real competitor page observations
- Brand tone examples
- Claims that must be avoided

Do not ask the skills to fill in missing certifications, sales data, ratings, reviews, test results, or platform approvals.

## 5. Output Review Process

Before publishing any output:

1. Check every claim against product documentation.
2. Remove unsupported certifications, awards, rankings, and performance claims.
3. Verify marketplace and ad platform rules.
4. Check trademarks and brand authorization.
5. Confirm offers, discounts, prices, and shipping details.
6. Review sensitive categories more carefully, including supplements, skincare, medical devices, children's products, pet food, and weight-loss products.

## 6. Safety Rules

Do not use this pack to:

- Generate fake reviews
- Generate fake certifications
- Fabricate sales volume
- Make medical treatment promises
- Make absolute guarantees
- Fake brand authorization
- Encourage review manipulation
- Create counterfeit, fake brand, or replica product content
- Promise marketplace approval, ad approval, ranking improvement, conversion improvement, or sales performance
