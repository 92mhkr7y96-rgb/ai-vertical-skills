# Competitor Product Analysis

Analyzes competitor product pages and turns observed gaps into actionable ecommerce opportunities.

## Suitable Scenarios

- Competitor page review
- Listing refresh
- Product differentiation
- Ad angle research
- Product image planning

## Steps

1. Paste competitor page details and review excerpts.
2. Add our product notes.
3. Run the skill.
4. Use recommendations without copying competitor content.

## Input Fields

Competitor name, title, price, platform, bullets, description, review excerpts, image notes, and our product notes.

## Output Fields

Positioning, assumed customer, selling points, pricing strategy, strengths, weaknesses, review opportunities, differentiation, listing suggestions, ad angles, and image opportunities.

## Common Mistakes

- Treating assumptions as confirmed facts
- Copying competitor language
- Inventing competitor review patterns

## Compliance Reminder

Use only provided competitor data and respect trademarks and copyrighted content.
