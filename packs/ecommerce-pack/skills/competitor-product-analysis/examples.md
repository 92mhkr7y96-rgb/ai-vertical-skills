# Competitor Product Analysis Examples

## Example 1: Physical Product

Input: Competing insulated water bottle listing with title, price, bullets, image notes, and review excerpts.

Output focus: positioning, strengths, weaknesses, review-based opportunities, listing changes, and image opportunities.

## Example 2: Digital Product

Input: Competing digital planner template listing with description, tags, price, preview image notes, and buyer comments.

Output focus: differentiation, pricing signal, page weaknesses, and Etsy listing opportunities.
