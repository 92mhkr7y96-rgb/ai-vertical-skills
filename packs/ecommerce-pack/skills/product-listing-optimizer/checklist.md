# Product Listing Optimizer Checklist

- [ ] Uses only provided product facts
- [ ] Translates features into benefits
- [ ] Separates functional and emotional benefits
- [ ] Fits the target platform
- [ ] Avoids fake certifications
- [ ] Avoids absolute guarantees
- [ ] Includes A/B variants
- [ ] Flags risky claims
- [ ] Does not fabricate reviews or sales numbers
