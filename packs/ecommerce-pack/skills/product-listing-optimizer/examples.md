# Product Listing Optimizer Examples

## Example 1: Physical Product

Input: Portable camping lantern for Amazon. Confirmed facts include rechargeable battery, hanging hook, adjustable brightness, and compact body. Restrictions: no waterproof claim and no exact battery-life claim.

Output focus: title, bullets, product description, FAQ, and claim-safe A/B title variants.

## Example 2: Digital Product

Input: Digital planner template for Etsy. Confirmed facts include PDF format, weekly planning pages, habit tracker, and printable layout. Restrictions: no income, productivity, or mental health guarantees.

Output focus: Etsy title options, benefit-led description, tag ideas, FAQ opportunities, and risky claims to avoid.
