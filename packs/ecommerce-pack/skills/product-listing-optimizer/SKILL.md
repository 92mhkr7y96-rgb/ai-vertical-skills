# Skill: product-listing-optimizer

## Purpose

Improve ecommerce listing copy by turning product facts into platform-aware titles, benefits, bullets, descriptions, keywords, FAQs, and test variants.

## Best for

Amazon, Shopify, Etsy, independent store, and marketplace sellers optimizing a product page.

## Not for

Inventing product capabilities, certifications, reviews, sales numbers, medical claims, or guaranteed results.

## Input

- Product name
- Product category
- Target platform
- Target customer
- Main use case
- Key features
- Materials/specs
- Price range
- Brand tone
- Competitor notes
- Language
- Compliance constraints

## Output

1. Product positioning summary
2. Optimized title
3. Short title alternatives
4. Key benefits
5. Feature-to-benefit conversion
6. Bullet points
7. Product description
8. Keyword suggestions
9. FAQ opportunities
10. Risky claims to avoid
11. A/B testing variants

## Process

1. Separate confirmed product facts from assumptions.
2. Translate features into functional and emotional benefits.
3. Match tone and structure to the target platform.
4. Draft title, bullets, description, keywords, FAQs, and variants.
5. Flag risky or unsupported wording.

## Quality Bar

- Converts features into user benefits.
- Separates functional and emotional selling points.
- Adapts to the target platform.
- Does not invent certifications or claims.
- Avoids absolute promises.
- Provides multiple A/B variants.

## Example Input

Product name: Portable camping lantern
Target platform: Amazon
Target customer: weekend campers and emergency preparedness buyers
Key features: rechargeable, hanging hook, adjustable brightness
Compliance constraints: do not claim waterproof certification or exact battery life

## Example Output

Optimized title: Rechargeable Camping Lantern with Hanging Hook, Adjustable Brightness for Camping, Car Trips, and Home Backup

Risky claims to avoid: waterproof, survival guaranteed, all-night battery life, certified safety rating unless documented.

## Safety / Compliance Notes

Use only provided facts. Do not fabricate reviews, certifications, ranking claims, platform approval, safety guarantees, or medical benefits.
