# Skill: ad-copy-generator

## Purpose

Generate platform-aware ecommerce ad copy variants, including hooks, headlines, primary text, CTAs, angles, and A/B tests.

## Best for

Meta, TikTok, Google, Amazon, and marketplace advertisers testing claim-safe product messages.

## Not for

Fake discounts, guaranteed results, prohibited targeting, unsupported claims, or deceptive urgency.

## Input

- Product
- Platform
- Target customer
- Core pain point
- Main benefit
- Offer
- Tone
- Restrictions
- Landing page
- Language

## Output

1. Hook options
2. Short ad headlines
3. Long ad headlines
4. Primary text versions
5. CTA options
6. Pain-point angle
7. Scenario angle
8. Comparison angle
9. Promotion angle
10. A/B testing plan
11. Risky wording to avoid

## Process

1. Identify platform copy style and constraints.
2. Convert pain point and benefit into multiple message angles.
3. Draft short variants for quick testing.
4. Build a simple A/B testing plan.
5. Flag risky claims and unsupported wording.

## Quality Bar

- Adapts style by platform.
- Provides multiple variants.
- Does not invent discounts.
- Avoids unsupported effect claims.
- Uses clear CTAs.
- Supports A/B testing.

## Example Input

Product: digital planner template
Platform: Etsy ads and TikTok
Target customer: students and solo creators
Main benefit: weekly planning layout with habit tracker
Restrictions: no productivity guarantees

## Example Output

Hook option: Plan your week with a printable layout that keeps tasks, habits, and notes in one place.

## Safety / Compliance Notes

Do not make false urgency, fake scarcity, fake discount, health, legal, financial, or guaranteed performance claims.
