# Ad Copy Generator Examples

## Example 1: Physical Product

Input: Insulated water bottle for Meta and TikTok. Benefit: fits commute, gym, and desk routines. Restriction: no temperature-duration claim unless provided.

Output focus: hooks, headlines, primary text, CTA options, and A/B testing plan.

## Example 2: Digital Product

Input: Digital planner template for Etsy ads. Benefit: printable weekly planning pages. Restriction: no productivity or income guarantees.

Output focus: Etsy-friendly hooks, short headlines, comparison angle, and risky wording to avoid.
