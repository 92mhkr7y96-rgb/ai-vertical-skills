# Review Analysis Skill Examples

## Example 1: Physical Product

Input: Pet hair remover review excerpts from positive, negative, and neutral reviews.

Output focus: positive themes, repeated complaints, improvement ideas, listing suggestions, ad angles, and FAQ opportunities.

## Example 2: Digital Product

Input: Digital planner template buyer comments about print quality, page layout, download instructions, and habit tracker usefulness.

Output focus: buyer motivations, common usage scenarios, support FAQ, listing improvements, and claims to avoid.
