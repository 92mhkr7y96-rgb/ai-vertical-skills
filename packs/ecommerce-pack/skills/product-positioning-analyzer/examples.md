# Product Positioning Analyzer Examples

## Example 1: Physical Product

Input: Pet hair remover for pet owners with sofas, car seats, and fabric surfaces. Competitors include sticky rollers and vacuum attachments.

Output focus: target profile, pain points, primary selling point, secondary selling points, and price positioning.

## Example 2: Digital Product

Input: Digital planner template for Etsy buyers who want printable weekly planning pages and habit tracking.

Output focus: buyer motivation, emotional value, differentiation from generic planners, and Etsy-friendly messaging angles.
