# Skill: product-positioning-analyzer

## 中文注释

这个 skill 用于产品定位分析，帮助明确目标用户、购买动机、核心痛点、差异化角度、主卖点和辅助卖点。输出应当为后续 Listing、广告和商品图片提供方向，但不能把未经验证的市场判断、评价、认证、销量或性能数据写成事实。

## Purpose

Clarify product positioning, target users, buying motivation, pain points, differentiation, and core selling points.

## Best for

New products, DTC brands, selection teams, and sellers preparing listing, ad, or image strategy.

## Not for

Market-size claims, revenue forecasts, legal advice, fake reviews, invented product claims, or unsupported competitor conclusions.

## Input

- Product
- Category
- Target customer
- Price
- Competitors
- Differentiators
- Use cases
- Brand tone
- Sales channel

## Output

1. Target customer profile
2. Buyer motivation
3. Core pain points
4. Functional value
5. Emotional value
6. Differentiation angle
7. Primary selling point
8. Secondary selling points
9. Price positioning
10. Recommended messaging angle
11. Risks and weak points

## Process

1. Identify the most specific buyer segment supported by the input.
2. Map use cases to pains and motivations.
3. Separate functional value from emotional value.
4. Select one primary selling point and 3-5 supporting points.
5. Suggest listing, ad, and image messaging angles.

## Quality Bar

- Defines a clear target customer.
- Explains the purchase reason.
- Provides one primary selling point.
- Provides 3-5 secondary selling points.
- Gives usable angles for listing, ads, and images.

## Example Input

Product: insulated water bottle
Category: drinkware
Target customer: commuters and gym users
Differentiators: slim cup-holder shape, leak-resistant lid, powder-coated finish

## Example Output

Primary selling point: everyday hydration that fits commute, gym, and desk routines without bulky gear.

## Safety / Compliance Notes

Separate facts from assumptions. Do not invent reviews, certifications, health outcomes, sales numbers, or performance metrics unless supplied.
