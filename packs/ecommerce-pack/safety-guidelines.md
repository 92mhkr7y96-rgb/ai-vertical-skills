# Safety Guidelines

This pack helps draft ecommerce content, but every output must be checked by a human before use.

## Rules

1. Do not generate fake product effects.
2. Do not fabricate certifications.
3. Do not fabricate sales volume.
4. Do not fabricate customer reviews.
5. Do not make medical treatment promises.
6. Do not make absolute guarantees.
7. Do not fake brand authorization.
8. Do not encourage review manipulation.
9. Do not generate counterfeit, fake brand, or replica product content.
10. Do not promise platform approval.
11. Users must verify platform policies, trademarks, certifications, and product claims.
12. Be extra careful with supplements, skincare, medical devices, children's products, pet food, and weight-loss products.

## Disclaimer

This skill pack helps draft, organize, and improve ecommerce content. It does not guarantee legal compliance, platform approval, ad approval, ranking improvement, conversion improvement, or sales performance. Users must verify all claims, certifications, trademarks, and platform policies before publishing.
