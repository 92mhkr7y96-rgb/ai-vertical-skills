# Ecommerce Skill Pack Use Cases

## 1. New Product Launch

Define positioning, analyze competitors, turn product facts into a listing, and prepare ad and image briefs.

## 2. Existing Listing Optimization

Improve titles, bullets, descriptions, keywords, FAQs, and conversion-focused messaging from provided product facts.

## 3. Competitor Research

Compare competitor positioning, selling points, pricing signals, page strengths, page weaknesses, and opportunity gaps.

## 4. Negative Review Retrospective

Extract recurring complaints from provided review excerpts and convert them into product, listing, FAQ, and image improvements.

## 5. Ad Creative Testing

Generate platform-aware hooks, headlines, primary text, CTA options, and A/B test variants.

## 6. Product Image Refresh

Create briefs for main images, lifestyle images, feature images, comparison images, size/spec images, and A+ content.

## 7. Independent Store Product Page Optimization

Use positioning, reviews, and competitor notes to improve Shopify or independent product pages.

## 8. Etsy Digital Product Launch

Create positioning, listing copy, and ad copy for templates, planners, printables, and other lightweight digital products.

## 9. Amazon Bullet Optimization

Convert features into benefits while avoiding unsupported claims and prohibited wording.

## 10. Xiaohongshu / Douyin Seeding Copy Support

Draft claim-safe angle ideas for short-form social commerce content using only provided product facts.
