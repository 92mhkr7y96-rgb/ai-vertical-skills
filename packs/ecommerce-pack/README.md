# Ecommerce Skill Pack

Ecommerce Skill Pack is an open-source AI workflow pack for ecommerce sellers who need reusable product, marketing, and merchandising workflows.

It is designed for Amazon, Shopify, Etsy, TikTok Shop, independent store, and social commerce operators. It helps turn product facts, market notes, competitor pages, and review excerpts into structured ecommerce outputs.

## Best For

- New product launches
- Existing listing optimization
- Competitor research
- Review analysis
- Ad copy exploration
- Product image brief planning

## Not For

- Creating fake reviews, fake certifications, fake sales numbers, or counterfeit content
- Making unsupported medical, health, legal, safety, or performance claims
- Guaranteeing marketplace approval, ad approval, ranking improvement, conversion improvement, or sales
